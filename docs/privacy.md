# SpacePeek Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-09

SpacePeek is a local-only macOS application developed by Fu Wang. This Privacy Policy explains how SpacePeek handles data.

## Data collection and processing

- SpacePeek does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data from users.
- All folder scanning and preview operations are performed locally on the user’s Mac inside a sandboxed process.
- The app does not make network connections, does not include analytics or advertising, and does not integrate third-party tracking SDKs.

## Crash reports and diagnostics

- SpacePeek does not include third-party crash-reporting or analytics services. If users opt in to send diagnostics through Apple system services, those reports are handled by Apple under Apple’s policies and user settings.
