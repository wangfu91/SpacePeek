SpacePeek Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Data Collection and Processing

SpacePeek scans folders locally and collects no personal data.

  • SpacePeek does not collect, transmit or store personal data from users.
  • Folder scanning and preview operations are performed locally on your Mac.
  • The app does not include analytics, advertising or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • Folder contents and scan results are not uploaded to remote servers.

Crash Reports and Diagnostics

SpacePeek does not include third-party crash-reporting services. If users opt in to send diagnostics through Apple system services, those reports are handled by Apple under Apple policies and user settings.

Support and Contact

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app behavior changes. We will update the effective date when material changes occur.
